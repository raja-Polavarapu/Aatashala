✨ Our Story

Where Nostalgia Meets Community

It started with 15 cousins — ages 16 to 35 — gathering every weekend to play the games we grew up with. 7 Stones. Ice Water. London London Stop. Games our grandparents played.

Neighbours started joining. Then something extraordinary happened — a video of us playing Kings Game went viral:

🔥 20 Million+ Instagram Views

The message was clear: people are starving for real, physical, joyful community. Screens have replaced the magic of the playground. We're here to bring it back.

Our trial session at My Home Tarkshya, Kokapet saw 72 kids show up. Every single parent gave positive feedback. Tarkshya called us back for more.

That's Aatashala — a cultural movement disguised as the best Saturday afternoon you've ever had.
